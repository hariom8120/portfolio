Hariom Ahirwar is a design professional specializing in educational book formatting, cover page design, and publishing-ready layouts. With experience at AISECT Bhopal, he works on creating structured, visually appealing, and professional learning materials that improve readability and presentation.

His design philosophy centres on clarity and purpose — every typographic choice, every margin, every colour serves the reader's experience. From print-ready PDFs to polished cover pages, Hariom brings precision and creativity to every project.
